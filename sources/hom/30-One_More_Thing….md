# One More Thing…

Please! You invested the price of this book plus perhaps eight hours of your time. At the risk of sounding like the author of a diet book, I would ask you to do something specific, and I leave you with a set of assignments. Choose what you like—but choose some—and perform them honestly.

You have trusted me enough to buy my book and read it. Now let me say a final thing: if you do at least 100 points worth of what you find here, you’ll be a distinctly better manager for it.

## ABOUT THE AUTHOR

  
Dr. Eli Goldratt’s book, The Goal has been a best seller since 1984 and is recognized as one of the best-selling management books of all time. Recently, the Japanese edition of The Goal  sold over 500,000 copies in less than one year after being released.  
  
Eli Goldratt is the author of many other books including the business novels, It’s Not Luck (the sequel to The Goal), Critical Chain, and Necessary but Not Sufficient. His books have been translated into 27 languages and sales have exceeded 6 million copies worldwide. His latest book is, Necessary but Not Sufficient, which focuses on the low rate of return obtained by companies on their huge investments in IT and enterprise resource planning (ERP) systems.   
  
Eli Goldratt is the founder of TOC for education; a non-profit organization dedicated to bringing TOC thinking and tools to teachers and their students ([www.tocforeducation.com](./http___www.tocforeducation.com)). Dr. Goldratt currently spends his time promoting TOC for Education and The Goldratt Group while he continues to write, lecture and consult.

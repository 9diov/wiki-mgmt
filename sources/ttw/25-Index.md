**About the Author**

**Jeffrey K. Liker** is Professor Emeritus, Industrial and Operations Engineering at The University of Michigan and President of Liker Lean Advisors, LLC. He has coauthored nine other books about Toyota including _The Toyota Way to Service Excellence, Designing the Future_, and _The Toyota Way to Lean Leadership_. A graphic novel with Eduardo Lander and Tom Root tells the story of lean transformation at a mail-order company: _Lean in a High-Variety Environment_. His articles and books have won thirteen Shingo Prizes for Research Excellence. He was inducted into the Association of Manufacturing Excellence Hall of Fame and the Shingo Academy.
